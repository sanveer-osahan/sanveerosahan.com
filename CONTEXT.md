# sanveerosahan.com

The personal site and portfolio of Sanveer Osahan. It hosts several **distinct content types** —
each a first-class kind of content with its own structure — rather than one undifferentiated blog.

## Language

### Content types

**Book Note**:
Sanveer's notes on a book he has read — the takeaways worth keeping, written as the body of the piece.
Carries the book's metadata: title, author, cover image, and an Amazon link. Grouped on the bookshelf by `tags`.
_Avoid_: Review, summary (the note is broader than a summary); Category (use Tags instead).

**Blog Post**:
A freeform written piece that isn't tied to a book or structured as a procedure.
_Avoid_: Article, essay.

**How-to Guide**:
A task-oriented piece that walks the reader through accomplishing a specific thing.
_Avoid_: Tutorial, post.

**Video**:
A YouTube video Sanveer has produced. Carries the YouTube link and, as its body, the **raw prep content**
used to make it (script, outline, research links). May optionally point to the piece it was derived from via `relatedTo`.
_Avoid_: Talk, recording.

**Raw prep content**:
The working material behind a Video — script, outline, links used while preparing it — kept as the Video's body.
_Avoid_: Script (too narrow), notes.

### Cross-cutting

**Tags**:
Freeform topical labels, many per piece. The only grouping mechanism across content types — there is no
separate "Category" concept; book-note shelves are grouped by tag.
_Avoid_: Category.
